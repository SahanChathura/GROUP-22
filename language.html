Improving Language Skills for Newcomers at KCGI

Language is the principal method of human communication, consisting of words used in a structured and conventional way and conveyed by speech, writing, or gesture. A study of the way children learn language. In Japan, we have to study two languages in particular. An English language is another Japanese language. Here we will discuss how to improve the language of new students coming to study at KCGI. For foreign students, there is not much problem in English language because we are studying from the school level. Even a simple effort can improve the English language.

However, the biggest problem here seems to be in Japanese language study. Because studies are conducted in English in the university and life here is very busy, it seems impossible to take a Japanese language physical class. Therefore, improving your Japanese language through self-study is a very wise thing to do. Here are essential and very important mobile apps for self-study, which you can download and run from the Play Store and App Store. Apps name like Japanese Dictionary Tokoboto, JKanji, Dash Japanese keyboard.
