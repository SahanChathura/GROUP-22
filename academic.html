Academic Guidance for Newcomers in KCGI

KCGI is an international environment with many international students. It aims to realize high-level IT education as a global education institution.

About KCGI

KCGI offers a superior curriculum leveraging the great variety of courses offered in IT and other special fields. It provides an opportunity for students to study IT in the English language, which is rare in a country like Japan where English language education is not as common. KCGI is the best guidance and great support for foreign students willing to improve their skills and change careers while working.

As a foreign student in Japan, life becomes busy with lectures and part-time jobs. KCGI has provided us online classes through which we can't miss our lectures.

Admission Requirements

Many students aspire to get admission to KCGI, making the competition high. Students willing to get into KCGI need to have a very good academic background. The admission requirements are as follows:

  • High school graduation
  • N5 level of Japanese ability
  • Strong English language proficiency
  • Some knowledge about lifestyles in Japan and Japanese language

Scholarship and Costs

KCGI can be a bit costly, but it provides scholarships to students, which has been of great help. Students willing to join KCGI should make themselves capable and work hard on both English and Japanese language proficiency. They should be prepared to face the struggles as foreign students in Japan.

KCGI's academic environment will lead you towards your career development and a successful life.
