Education Support for Newcomers to the KCGI

A Project to Provide Educational Resources, Guidance and Support to Newcomers Who Join to KCGI.

Hello and a warm welcome to our student group's web page!

We are thrilled to have you here and eager to share our journey with you. Whether you're a fellow student, educator, or simply someone interested in exploring the world of academia and student life, you've come to the right place!

Who We Are

Orientation and Information

We offer orientation sessions and information materials to help newcomers get familiar with the KCGI and its educational system.

Academic Guidance

We provide academic guidance and counseling to help newcomers plan their courses, set their goals, and overcome any challenges.

Language Support

We offer language support and tutoring to help newcomers improve their English or Japanese skills and communicate effectively.

Online Learning Platform

We provide access to an online learning platform where newcomers can find educational resources, courses, and tools to enhance their learning experience.

Cultural Integration

We organize cultural events and activities to help newcomers learn about the Japanese culture and society and make new friends.
